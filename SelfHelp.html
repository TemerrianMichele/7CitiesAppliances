Clothes Not Drying? Time For A Dryer Vent Cleaning
If your clothes are not drying in the dryer, do a dryer vent cleaning. Dryer vent cleaning is not rocket science, here's how to do it. If your towels and clothes take a long time to dry in the dryer, the easiest and cheapest repair to do first is to clean out the dryer duct pipe. Lint clogs up the dryer vent, preventing the wet air from being vented outside, so your clothes don't get dry.
